About Us

InTakeIQ is designed to make nutrition tracking easy, fast and personalized. Our website allows students to search through food items through a real-time API, enter the exact number of grams they consumed, calculate the amount of calories and build a clear overview of their "Today's Intake". Once their list is complete, they can then generate an AI summary that offers helpful insight to what they should do next to get to their goal! Our mission is to help students track what they eat on a daily basis and offer them tips to reach a healthier and/or desired weight goal.
